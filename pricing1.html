Pricing Information

~How did you hear about us? It could save you money! ~

Annual Membership Fee:* $25 per child
$40 per family of 2 or more children
Minimum 3 rides/week:** $14/per ride one-way
Late Payment Fee: $25.00 per month after the 5th
Babysitter/Caretaker No-show: $10.00 per incident (if driver must return to attempt drop off again)
NSF Check: $30.00 per check
Clean-up Fee: $25.00 per accident/illness in vehicle for commercial cleaning
Rescheduling Fee: $10.00 for MAJOR itinerary change; DOES NOT APPLY TO CANCELLATIONS
Ride Cancellation: $10.00 per ride if not received by 7pm the previous business day


Payment Terms:

Prepayment Required: Full payment must be prepaid monthly by cash, check, Mastercard, Visa, or American Express at the end of each month for the following month's transportation service. A $25.00 late fee will be assessed if payment is received after the 5th of the month. Service will be interrupted without further notice if payment is not received by the 5th of the month. Your payment will be reflected on your following monthly bill once received.

If you plan on using last-minute services, we require a credit card # on file for billing purposes.

All prepaid transportation is non-refundable.

*Membership good thru current school year (September thru June).
*Cost covers registration and guarantees a seat when your child needs it.

**Prices based on destinations within the Livonia Public School area. A 5% surcharge will apply for destinations outside Livonia.
**Additional children in family with same pick up and drop off locations and times receive 20% discount per additional child.
