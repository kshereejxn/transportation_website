Welcome To Our Web Page

Does your child's schedule conflict with yours?
Wish your child had a way to after school activities?


Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC provides door-to-door transportation service for school-aged children*. Our services are geared for children 4-12 years old but we will transport older children by request.

Routine or last-minute pick-up/drop-offs - That’s what we do! Our mission is “To improve the quality of life for children by providing them safe and reliable transportation from one location to another” and that is what we intend to do.
We offer a safe and reliable alternative for you "The Parent" to get your child where they need to be when they need to be there.

We transport:

  • To/from local daycare centers,
  • To/from local schools,
  • After school activities,
  • To/from Scout meetings,
  • Or just about anywhere in your local area!

With Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC, you get convenience and peace of mind. Save gas and time by letting us get your child where they need to be when they need to be there.

We currently service: The Livonia Public School District Area

Not in your area? Give us a call. We are looking to expand our service area.


Thank you for riding with us!

*We are not handicap accessible at this time and apologize for the inconvenience. We hope to correct this as soon as possible.
