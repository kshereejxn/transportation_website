Staff Information

Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC is transportation service for school-aged children based out of Livonia, MI.

Katrine S. Jackson, Managing Director, thought of the idea of helping busy parents out while living in California. She noticed that some children were missing out on great opportunities to excel in after-school activities due to their parent(s) busy schedule. That is when she researched the idea of providing transportation for children and introduced it to the area she now calls home, Livonia.

Currently, KS Jackson is the only driver for Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC. As more members come aboard, their profiles will be posted.
