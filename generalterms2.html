General Terms and Agreement

Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC will provide scheduled transportation pursuant to the following terms and conditions:
  1. Upon acceptance of this AGREEMENT, Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC agrees to provide transportation to and/or from the designated locations and dates of service as stated on the registration form. Transportation shall be provided in accordance with the rules and regulations applicable in City of Livonia and the State of Michigan.
  2. Although Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC will always strive for on-time performance some unpredictable factors (bad traffic accidents, weather, children that are late, etc.) may cause delays on occasion. Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC will do our best to be on-time and provide a phone call to the place of pick-up/drop-off if the delay is more than 10 minutes.
  3. Any time that your child will not require transportation as scheduled, all ride cancellations must be made by 7pm the previous business day to avoid being charged. For each failure to provide this notification, there will be a $10.00 service charge on the following month's invoice.
  4. All AGREEMENT terminations are subject to a two-week (10 business days) written notice by the parent/legal guardian authorized to make changes. This notice can be given to the driver or mailed to the office. Upon receipt of such notification, the services will cease at requested date of time.
  5. Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC will observe the same holidays and in-service days as individual schools in the area. If you need service during these days, this option is available to you on an individual basis. Snow days will be strictly followed along the guidelines of each school’s closing. For the safety of the children, Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC will not operate in dangerous driving conditions.
  6. All services are prepaid and nonrefundable. We cannot provide refunds or credit for absences, cancellations, holidays, school breaks, sick days, expulsions or suspensions. All fees are prepaid and non-refundable.
  7. Our cost-effective rates are based on group transportation. We will do our utmost to provide reasonable drive times, but our vans are very busy during the hours of 6am-9am & 3pm-7pm. Drive times will range from 10 minutes to 30 minutes depending on your location. All pickup and drop off times are estimates and subject to vary as our schedules change.
  8. Due to consideration for all our customers, maximum wait time at each stop is two minutes. Please have rider ready and waiting OUTSIDE for our van to ensure on-time arrival at school/activity. We will depart AFTER SOUNDING HORN ONCE.
  9. At no time will Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC leave the vehicle unattended. All children must have an authorized authority to receive them at time of drop-off.
  10. If for some reason, Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC fails to pick up your child due to an unplanned or emergency situation, you will be contacted and then credited with that absence on your next statement. If we are unable to continue to provide transportation for any reason, you will receive notice 10 business days prior to any change being implemented.
  11. If your child rides with the Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC more times than this AGREEMENT specified, you will be billed for the additional trips at the applicable rates.
  12. If it becomes necessary to enforce any of the payment provisions of this AGREEMENT, then the prevailing party will recover all reasonable attorney fees and court costs from the non-prevailing party. In the event that the client defaults in the payment of fees due under the terms of this AGREEMENT for a period that exceeds 30 days, subsequently, the due dates of all payments may be accelerated by Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC and all remaining payments due under the AGREEMENT will be due and payable immediately.
  13. Seat belts or child restraint chairs are required for all passengers in Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC vehicles. Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC reserves the right to terminate transportation for any child that does not cooperate with the use of seat belts or car seats. In accordance with state law, children under 4 are required to be in child restraint seats.
  14. Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC is not liable for the health, safety or welfare of your child once your child is dropped at the agreed destination. Any damage to a Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC vehicle caused by a rider is the responsibility of the parent.
  15. Due to rising fuel costs, a fuel surcharge up to 5% of your monthly fee may be charged if fuel prices exceed $3.00 per gallon.
  16. At no time will eating or drinking be allowed in the vehicle.
  17. Audio/video devices may be used with headphones at a reasonable listening level.
  18. Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC reserves the right to cancel this AGREEMENT without notice at any time due to disciplinary problems with a child while riding in a Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC vehicle.
  19. Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC reserves the right to cancel, alter, and/or change the terms of this AGREEMENT at any time.
  20. Kids Door 2 Door Service, LLC reserves the right to change rates without further notice.
